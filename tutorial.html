Tutorial

Welcome to Tic Tac Shape! This is a game that's similar to Tic Tac Toe, but with a twist - instead of X's and O's, you'll be playing with shapes! Here's how to play:

  1. Choose who you want to play with - you can play with a friend, or against the computer.
  2. Choose your shape - you can choose from squares, circles, and triangles.
  3. Start playing! The game board is a 3x3 grid, and you and your opponent will take turns placing your shapes on the board. The goal is to get three of your shapes in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  4. If you're playing against the computer, you can choose the difficulty level - easy, medium, or hard. Good luck!
